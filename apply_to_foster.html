Fostering Application

Please make sure to type SHELTIE FOSTERING in the subject line of your e-mail. Copy this application, paste it into a new email to us, answer the questions, and send.

Name:

Full Address (city, state, zip):

List your occupation and whether you are full-time or part-time:

Home Phone:

Work Phone:

E-mail:

1) Have you ever owned a Sheltie before? And do you still have that dog?

2) What happened to your last dog?

3) During the last five years (see below):

  • Have you lost a pet (not through death)?
  • Have you had one poisoned?
  • Have you had an animal killed by a vehicle?
  • Have you had an animal die due to disease? If yes, what did it die of?

4) Do you have any other animals (list names, type, age, sex and whether they are neutered/spayed):

5) Please list everyone (including yourself) who lives in your household and their ages:

6) Please indicate whether you live in a house, apartment, condo, or trailer:

7) Do you rent or own?

8) If you rent, do you have the landlords permission to keep a dog?
If so, please list your landlord's name and phone number:

9) Do you have a yard and if so, is it fenced?
What type of fencing?

10) Do all family adults work and is someone home during the day?
Please explain:

11) Do you intend to keep this dog primarily indoors or outdoors?
Where will it sleep?

12) Do you have a sex preference?

13) Would you consider an older dog? To what age?

14) Are other members of your household aware that you are considering fostering a Sheltie?

15) Are you planning to move in the near future?

16) Is anyone in your house allergic to animals?

17) Are you familiar with animal control regulations in your area?

18) Are you willing to allow a Rescue representative member to visit your home by appointment?

19) How did you hear about South Carolina Sheltie Rescue?

20) Please provide the name and number of any vets used in the past 5 years:

By sending this e-mail I am attesting to the truthfulness of my answers.

NOTE: If you are under 18 years of age, a parent or guardian must also sign this application. If you have any questions, or if we can be of assistance, please do not hesitate to e-mail.

We reserve the right to refuse any applicant.
