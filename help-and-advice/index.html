Expert Help and Advice

Phyllis Beasley is past co-founder of South Carolina Sheltie Rescue. She is a certified professional dog trainer (CPDT-KA) through the Council for the Certification of Professional Dog Trainers. She is an assistant trainer with Pawsitive Results, LLC, and a volunteer trainer for the Greater Columbia Obedience Club. Phyllis also volunteers her training skills with SC Sheltie Rescue to help foster and adoptive homes, including writing helpful articles addressing many issues that arise with sheltie ownership or fostering. Please check back for more articles to come!
