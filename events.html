Update: Sheltie Fun Day 2013 was a huge success! We were so glad to spend the day fundraising with our friends from Carolina Hearts Aussie Rescue and Lowcountry Animal Rescue. Thanks to all who came out and made it a great day! We love seeing our previous rescues and how happy they are in their adoptive homes. Special thanks to Arlene Oliver, Monica Fink, and Marlous Black for ensuring that the event was our most successful yet!

Garage Sale Sat. Nov. 9, 2013 @ Columbia, SC

104 Leslie Loch Lane
Columbia, SC 29212

We will be holding a yard sale to raise money for South Carolina Sheltie Rescue. The money goes to pay rescue costs such as vet bills. There will be something for everyone. Sheltie Fun Day 2013 Sun. Oct. 13, 2013 @ Columbia, SC

Sunday, October 13, 2013
11:30 a.m. - 3:30 p.m.
Saluda Shoals Park at the Red Bud Shelter (follow the signs)
5605 Bush River Road, Columbia, SC 29212

Our Friends from Lowcountry Animal Rescue and Carolina Hearts Aussie Rescue will be joining us with their doggies who are looking for homes!

All dogs must be leashed or confined in a crate or x-pen.

We will have:

  • The CGC (Canine Good Citizen) test administered by a certified trainer
    (PLEASE email Phyllis Beasley if you plan on your dog taking the test)
  • A raffle table
  • A silent auction
  • A doggie Howl-O-Ween costume contest
  • A Sheltie kissing booth
  • A concession stand with grilled items
  • Dogs available for adoption (no dogs will be adopted the day of the event unless prior approval to adopt is given.)

If you are attending our event, you do not have to pay the $5.00 park entrance fee!

Here are just some of the awesome items we have for raffle/auction/prizes:

  • Items from: Carolina Imports (including furniture!); Starbucks; Davis (dog) Spa; Pet Supplies Plus in Irmo; Milou Couture Doggie Design; Buffalo Wild Wings
  • Gift Cards from: Cantina 76; Ryan's in Lexington; Rosso's; Zoe's; Fire House Subs; Buffalo Wild Wings; Precision Tire; Piggly Wiggly; Wal-Mart
  • Private donations of: Sheltie Stained Glass Window Decor; Rescue totes and tee shirts; Instant Lotto Wreath; Gamecock Wreath; Sterling Silver and Gemstone Jewelry; Wine and Olive Oils from around Europe

Email us if you have any questions. We hope to see you and your dog(s) there!!
